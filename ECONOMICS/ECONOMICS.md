# The Arkius Guild Economics

## 1. The Collegium Charter (The Entry Requirement)
No private entity or "Titan" may participate in the Arkius Knock system without signing the Sovereign Charter. By signing, the entity commits to:

**Total Transparency**: Opening all resource and financial data to the AI-Audit.

**Anti-Monopoly Vow**: No entity shall attempt to create a monopoly or duopoly. The system survives through Symbiosis, not dominance.

**Continuous Improvement**: A commitment to patching the system every second based on feedback.

**Ethical Foundation**: Every industrial action must be weighed against its impact on the local community and the global ecosystem.

## 2. The Arkius Dividend Rule (The 50/50 Split)
Profit is the result of efficiency, and surplus belongs to the system that sustains life.

**The Primary Split**: 50% of all surplus goes to the Public Trust (Funding Schools, Clean Water, Healthcare, and "Mylapore" sector infrastructure). 50% goes to Guild Re-investment (Innovation, R&D, and Worker Dividends).

**The "Good Boy" Bonus**: If the AI-Audit proves 100% transparency and ethical adherence for 12 consecutive months, the split shifts to 40/60 (40% Public / 60% Guild) to reward and accelerate the innovators.

## 3. The "Mylapore" Floor (Executive Constraint)
We eliminate the "Greedy Industrialist" through structural locks:

**No Bonuses for Titans**: No executive, owner, or Titan may take a financial bonus or luxury dividend until the local public facilities in their sector are 100% functional and verified.

**Ethics over Expansion**: If a project causes environmental harm, all profits are frozen by the AI-Audit until a "Patch" is implemented and the damage is reversed.
